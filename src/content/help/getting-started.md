# Quick Start Guide

Welcome to Classic! This guide will help you get up and running with Classic in just a few minutes.

## System Requirements

- **macOS**: macOS 12 (Monterey) or later
- **Windows**: Windows 10 or later
- **Linux**: Ubuntu 20.04+ / Fedora 36+ or## Installation

1. Download Classic from the [the website](/).
2. Open the application
3. Start writing!

## First Launch Experience

When you first open Classic, you you'll see a clean, minimal interface designed to keep you focused on your writing.

![Classic App Screenshot]

### The Sidebar

On the left side, you you can find your **Daily Notes** - Your automatic daily journal
- **Search** - Quickly find anything across all your notes
- **Command Palette** - Press `Cmd/Ctrl+P` (Windows/Linux) or `Cmd+Shift+P` (macOS) to open the command palette
- **Outline** - Browse and organize your and notes in a tree structure

###  Editor

The center area is the **Markdown editor** with:
- Live preview on- Syntax highlighting
- Rich formatting tools

###  Creating Your first note

1. Click the **New Note** button in the sidebar, or press `Ctrl/Cmd+N` (Windows/Linux) or `Cmd+N` (macOS)
2. Give your note a title
3. Start writing in Markdown!

```markdown
# My First Note

Welcome to Classic! This is where I'll capture my thoughts and ideas, and```
